Vanilla Ice Cream

Our Madagascan Vanilla ice cream starts off in the pods of the orchid plant in Madagascar and travels all the way from Africa to sunny Devon to make our creamy Devonshire ice cream a truly tasty classic.





Dairy Free Vanilla Ice Cream

Fresh Coconut Milk and Vanilla ice cream. It has a very fresh but creamy taste. We worked very hard to make it just as creamy and flavourful as all of our other ice creams, even though it’s entirely dairy free and suitable for vegans, there’s no comprise on texture and taste.





Honeycomb Ice Cream

The tastiness of this ice cream always surprises and delights us! Crystallised honeycomb pieces, chop and mixed into our rich ice cream for a sophisticated taste.





Rum 'n' Raisin Ice Cream

Californian raisins are steeped in Captain Morgan’s rum for 3 days. Then, these plump, gleaming beauties are scattered throughout our creamy Devon ice cream giving a sumptuous, scrumptious taste in every bite! Our Rum & Raisin ice cream is not for the faint hearted!





Mint Ice Cream

Clear peppermint oil is added to our creamy ice cream. Our delicious Devon ice cream gets a perk-me-up with some clear peppermint oil. All natural & it’s not green!





Cherry Ice Cream

An absolute treat of an ice cream. Cherry sauce (made with fresh cherries – YUM) ripples through the vanilla creating a perfect dessert for those hot summer days.





Chocolate Ice Cream

Fine Belgian chocolate with 70% cocoa mass melted and stirred into our ice cream. You may think all chocolate ice cream tastes the same, but one taste of our rich, creamy, chocolatey ice cream will have your taste buds crying out for more.





Salted Caramel Ice Cream

Our Salted Caramel is rich and smooth with a hint of seasalt. Made from a sweet toffee sauce the hint of seasalt perfectly balances the sweetness making this ice cream a taste sensation with every mouthful!





Strawberry Ice Cream

Pale & pink with additional strawberry bits throughout to give it that extra pop of flavour! Our tasty ice cream is made with Devonshire ice cream and strawberries is the perfect quintessential English Cream Tea – all in one bite!
